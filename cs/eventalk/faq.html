The conference is on a business day. I can only attend part of the conference. Should I still register for a seat?

We advise you to make appropriate arrangements with your team such that you and your team can attend the conference in full and remain uninterrupted by the business. In the rare circumstance such an arrangement cannot be made then it is more appropriate to not attend.
