6th June 2018, 6:30pm to 9:00pm

Asim Hussain talks about "Everyday AI"

In this fast-paced talk, Asim Hussain will open your mind to what's possible by demoing several AI-powered JavaScript apps, from sentiment analysis, through conversational bots to image recognition and show you how to build them using readily available APIs. By the end, you'll walk away with the knowledge, inspiration and drive to create your very own AI-powered web application.

Asim is a developer, trainer and speaker with over 16 years experience working for organisations such as the European Space Agency, Google and now Microsoft, where he is a Senior Cloud Developer Advocate. He's also author of the book and video series "Angular: From Theory to Practice" (now free!) and has taught over 50,000 students Angular, JavaScript and Ionic through his site codecraft.tv.
